Expert content (compact by default)

Semaglutide is a once-weekly GLP-1 receptor agonist that improves glycemic control and supports weight management. It binds to the GLP-1 receptor to trigger glucose-dependent insulin release while slowing gastric emptying.

  • Common brands: Ozempic®, Wegovy®, Rybelsus®.
  • Molecular formula: C187H291N45O59.
  • Typical storage: 2–8 °C with validated cold chain.
  • Subcutaneous injections and pre-filled pen cartridges.
  • Oral tablets formulated with absorption enhancers.
  • Depot injections and specialty CDMO programs.

Specify salt form (base vs acetate), desired pharmacopeia, and cold-chain packaging in RFQs.

  • Request EU/US/WHO GMP certificates and recent audit summaries.
  • Common document bundle: CoA, DMF or ASMF, impurity profile, stability data.
  • Ask for GDP-compliant cold-chain logistics and batch genealogy.

Originator production is centered in Denmark, while specialized peptide CDMOs across Europe and North America offer tech-transfer and fill-finish capacity. China and India continue scaling peptide synthesis with improved lyophilization capabilities.

Lead times can exceed 16 weeks; share forecasts early to reserve peptide slots.
